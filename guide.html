Superpower for Gemini Manual

Learn how to make the most out of your Gemini experience.

← Show Full Manual

Superpower Menu

The command center for your extension. Click the Superpower for Gemini header in the sidebar to reveal or hide your Folders, Prompt Library, and Settings. Keep your interface clean by collapsing it when not in use.

Folders & Management

Keep your workspace organized. Manage your conversations with custom folders, a dedicated chats menu, and a safe trash bin. Press Alt + F to open/close instantly.

📁 Folders

Create custom folders and subfolders to group your conversations by project, topic, or priority.

💬 Chats Menu

Access all your conversations from a centralized menu. Quickly search, filter, add to folders, delete and navigate through your chat history.

🗑️ Trash Bin

Deleted a chat by mistake? No worries. The Trash Bin stores your deleted conversations, allowing you to restore them whenever needed.

Prompts Library

Build your own personal library of reusable templates. Whether it's a simple email structure or a complex coding workflow, save it once and access it instantly. Press Alt + P to open/close instantly.

⚡️ Prompts

Type // in the chat box to access single-step templates. Use variables like {{topic}} to fill in details dynamically when you use them. You can also reference other prompts using [[prompt name]] to build modular templates.

🔗 Chains

Type ,, to access Prompt Chains. These are multi-step workflows. The extension sends the first prompt, waits for Gemini to finish generating, and automatically sends the next step in the sequence. NEW: Each step can now optionally switch the AI model (Fast/Thinking/Pro) or select a tool (Deep Research, Create Images, etc.) before execution using the buttons next to each step.

🚀 Quick Actions

Configure up to 5 custom Quick Action buttons that appear directly above the chat input area. Assign any prompt or chain to a button for one-click access to your most-used workflows. Each button can have a custom icon and a shortcut.

Settings in Sidebar

Access your controls instantly without leaving the chat. Click on the settings icon in the sidebar to open the settings menu. Press Alt + S to open/close instantly.

Export Button

Save your conversations instantly. You can export chats to PDF, Word (.docx), plain Text, or even directly Print them or Copy them to the clipboard.

Info Button & Notes

Get detailed statistics about your current chat (Date created, number of messages, ID). You can also attach private notes to any conversation for future reference.

Wide Chat

Expand the message area to use the full width of your screen instead of the narrow centered column. Toggle it on and off using the ↔ button in the top-right header bar (to the left of the Export button).

Prompt Optimizer

Turn simple thoughts into professional prompts. Click "Optimize" to have AI rewrite your request for better clarity, context, and results.

Word/Character Counter

Track your input length in real-time. Click the counter to expand it and view detailed statistics like sentence count, paragraphs, lines, and characters (with/without spaces).

Daily Prompt Counter

Keep track of your daily usage per model. The counter shows how many prompts you've sent using the currently selected model (Fast, Thinking, or Pro) today. The icon changes to match the active model. Hover over the counter to see counts for all models. The count resets automatically at midnight.

📡 Sync Across Devices: Enable syncing in the Daily Counter settings (gear icon) to keep your count synchronized across all your devices via Chrome Sync. When enabled, your daily count stays consistent whether you're on your desktop, laptop, or any other device using the same Chrome profile with the extension installed.

Scroll Buttons

Navigate long threads effortlessly. Use the floating arrows to jump instantly to the very top or bottom of the conversation. Double-click the down arrow to enable auto-scroll and automatically follow new messages.

Download Audio

Save AI responses as audio files. Click the download button next to the Copy button on any AI response to download it as an OGG audio file. The feature uses Gemini's built-in text-to-speech to generate the audio on demand.

Collapsible Pinned Chats

Reclaim vertical space in your sidebar. Collapse the Pinned chats to make room for more unpinned chats in your sidebar.

Drag Chat to Attach

Quickly reference a previous conversation by dragging it from the sidebar onto the input area. This generates a PDF of the dragged chat and attaches it as a file, so Gemini can read and reference the full conversation context.

How to use

  1. Locate the chat you want to reference in the sidebar.
  2. Click and drag it onto the text input area.
  3. The chat content is extracted and a PDF is generated.
  4. The PDF is automatically attached to your current conversation.

Pro feature — This feature is available exclusively to Pro users. Free users will see an upgrade prompt when attempting to use it.

Hide Premade Buttons

Declutter your new chat view. This option hides Google's premade action buttons like "Create image", "Write anything", "Create video", and other suggestions that appear under the input field when starting a new conversation.

Hide Upgrade Buttons

Remove the "Upgrade to Google AI Plus", "Upgrade to Google AI Pro", and "Upgrade to Google AI Ultra" upsell buttons from your Gemini interface. These buttons appear both in new chats and inside opened conversations. Toggle this setting to hide them for a cleaner experience.

Hide Location Info

Maximize your sidebar real estate. Hiding the location info at the bottom frees up space to display more chats in your sidebar.

Hide Activity

Remove the "Activity" button from the bottom of the sidebar to gain extra vertical space for your chats.

Hide Gems (Master Toggle)

Completely hide the Gems interface to remove distractions and maximize the space available for your chats in sidebar.

Hide Premade Gems (Gallery)

Hide Google's default premade gems from the main view when you open the Gem Manager, keeping it focused on your custom creations.

Hide Premade Gems (Sidebar)

Remove the default premade gems from the sidebar navigation. This clears up significant space for your custom gems and chats in sidebar.

Hide Custom Gems

Hide all gems you created from both the Gem Gallery and the sidebar. Useful if you want to keep the interface clean while still keeping your custom gems saved for later use.

📍 Location: This is a subsetting of Hide Gems — click the gear icon (⚙️) next to the Hide Gems toggle to reveal it.

Message Queue

Never stop typing. Superpower for Gemini allows you to queue up multiple messages while the AI is still generating, ensuring you never lose your flow.

⏳ Smart Queue

If Gemini is busy, simply type your next message and hit Enter. The extension automatically captures it and sends it the instant Gemini is ready. You can queue as many prompts as you need.

📋 Queue Window

Stay in control. When messages are pending, a floating status window appears in the bottom right corner. It allows you to see exactly what is waiting and lets you cancel individual messages if you change your mind.

Message Timestamps ⚠️

Experimental Feature: This feature is still being refined and may not work perfectly on all chats.

Display timestamps next to each message showing when it was sent. For new messages, the exact time is recorded. For older messages opened after install, timestamps show "--:--" since the original time is unknown.

Note: This feature is disabled by default. Enable it in Settings → Experimental to try it out.

Free vs Pro ✨

Superpower for Gemini is free to use with generous limits. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited access and exclusive features.

🆓 Free Tier

All core features are available for free, with the following usage limits:

  • Folders — Up to 2 folders
  • Custom Prompts — Up to 2 saved prompts
  • Prompt Chains — Up to 2 saved chains
  • Optimize Prompt — 3 uses per day
  • Export Chat — 2 exports per day
  • Message Queue — Up to 1 queued message at once
  • Quick Action Buttons — Up to 2 enabled at once

👑 Pro Tier

Unlock unlimited access to everything:

  • Unlimited folders, prompts, chains, prompt optimizations, exports, and queue
  • Chat Referencing — Drag chats from the sidebar into the text input field to attach them as context
  • Custom Optimization Prompt — Write your own system prompt for the Optimizer
  • Google Drive Sync — Sync your folders, prompts, and chains across devices via your own Google Drive
  • All future Pro features included

🔑 Activating Pro

After purchasing, you'll receive a license key. To activate:

  • Click the Upgrade button (crown icon) in the extension settings header
  • Click "I have a license key" in the upgrade modal
  • Paste your license key and click Activate
  • You can use one license key on up to 3 devices

Note: Daily limits reset at midnight local time. Permanent limits apply until you upgrade.

Google Drive Sync

Google Drive Sync ✨

Pro Feature. Keep your Superpower data in sync across every device you use Gemini on. Drive Sync uploads your folders, custom prompts, and prompt chains to a private app folder in your own Google Drive, then automatically pulls in changes made on your other browsers.

What gets synced:

  • Folders and folder assignments
  • Custom prompts in your Prompt Library
  • Prompt chains

How to enable it:

  • Open the Superpower settings (sidebar or extension icon)
  • Find Google Drive Sync and toggle it on
  • Sign in with the Google account you want to use for sync and approve access
  • Repeat on any other device — your data will merge automatically

🔒 Privacy: Sync uses a dedicated app folder in your own Google Drive. Your data never touches our servers — it goes directly between your browser and Google.

Native Settings Integration

A permanent entry point injected directly into Gemini's internal "Settings & Help" menu. This option cannot be hidden, ensuring you always have a "Fail-Safe" way to access your Superpower settings—even if you've accidentally hidden the sidebar shortcut or the extension icon.

Anonymous Statistics

Help us improve Superpower for Gemini by sharing anonymous usage data. When enabled, the extension collects basic, non-identifiable information about which features are used and how often.

🔒 Your Privacy is Protected

  • Completely anonymous — No personal data, emails, or accounts are ever collected
  • No chat content — We never see your prompts, responses, or conversations
  • Random ID only — Each installation gets a random UUID that cannot be linked to you
  • Feature usage only — We only track which features are used (e.g., "Optimize button clicked")
  • Opt-out anytime — Disable this setting to stop all data collection immediately

📊 What We Collect

Only anonymous usage statistics like:

  • Extension installed/updated events
  • Feature toggle states (on/off)
  • Feature usage counts (e.g., how often Optimize is used)
  • No personally identifiable information (PII) ever

Keyboard Shortcuts

Master Gemini with speed. Use these keyboard shortcuts to navigate and control the extension without touching your mouse. You can customize them in the extension settings under "Keyboard Shortcuts".

⌨️ Quick Focus

Keep your hands on the keyboard. Press the / (forward slash) key at any time to instantly move your cursor to the chat input box.

🪄 Optimize Prompt

Instantly improve your prompt's quality. Press Alt + O to trigger the Prompt Optimizer for your current input.

✓ Fix Grammar

Quickly fix spelling and grammar errors in your text. Press Alt + G to send your input to AI for correction without changing the meaning or tone.

📂 Quick Open

Instantly open menus without clicking:

  • Alt + F — Open Folders
  • Alt + P — Open Prompt Library
  • Alt + S — Open Settings

🔄 Switch Model

Instantly switch between Gemini models without using the menu:

  • Ctrl + Alt + F — Switch to Fast mode
  • Ctrl + Alt + T — Switch to Thinking mode
  • Ctrl + Alt + P — Switch to Pro mode

🛠️ Switch Tool

Quickly switch between different Gemini tools to change the type of output or interaction:

  • Alt + N — Switch to No Tool
  • Alt + I — Switch to Image (Create image)
  • Alt + C — Switch to Canvas
  • Alt + R — Switch to Deep Research
  • Alt + V — Switch to Video

🚀 Open Gemini

Quickly open Gemini in a new tab from anywhere. Press Alt + Shift + G to launch Gemini. This shortcut works globally, even when you're not on the Gemini page.

📌 Toggle Sidebar

Quickly show or hide the Gemini sidebar. Press Alt + Q to toggle the sidebar visibility without using your mouse.

💡 Open Custom Instructions

Navigate directly to your custom instructions page. Press Ctrl + Alt + I to instantly open the custom instructions editor. This shortcut is disabled by default—enable it in the Keyboard Shortcuts settings.

➕ Open New Chat

Instantly start a fresh conversation. Press Ctrl + Alt + N to open a new chat window, preserving your language settings (like hl=cs). This shortcut is disabled by default—enable it in the Keyboard Shortcuts settings.

Model Settings

Customize which AI model is used for specific features and set a default model when opening Gemini.

🚀 Default Model

Automatically switch to your preferred model when opening Gemini:

  • None — Don't switch (keep last used model)
  • Fast — Always start with Fast mode
  • Thinking — Always start with Thinking mode
  • Pro — Always start with Pro mode

🤖 Optimization Model

Choose which Gemini model to use when you click the "Optimize" button or press Alt + O:

  • Fast — Quick optimization with Fast mode
  • Thinking — Slower optimization with Thinking mode
  • Pro — Slowest optimization with Pro mode

🔒 Temporary Chat Model

Automatically switch to your preferred model when starting a temporary chat:

  • None — Don't switch (use default Fast mode)
  • Fast — Use Fast mode for temporary chats
  • Thinking — Use Thinking mode for temporary chats
  • Pro — Use Pro mode for temporary chats

Access these settings by clicking Configure next to "Model Settings" in the Advanced section of your extension settings (popup or sidebar).
