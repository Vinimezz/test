Welcome to Superpower for Gemini

Thank you for installing Superpower for Gemini! Superpower for Gemini runs entirely on your device to give you the missing power-user features you've been waiting for.

Current Features
📂 Native Folders
Organize your chats into custom folders. Drag and drop to sort and declutter your sidebar instantly.
🔗 Prompt Chains
Create multi-step workflows. The extension automatically sends the next prompt after Gemini finishes generating.
📝 Prompt Library
Save your best prompts and access them instantly in the chat using the // shortcut.
✨ AI Optimizer
One-click AI optimization turns simple phrases into professional, high-quality prompts.
📤 Universal Export
Export your chats to PDF, Word, or Text. Save your work and share it easily.
⏳ Message Queue
Don't wait for Gemini. Queue up your next thoughts, and we'll send them automatically.
⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts
Master Gemini with speed. Use / to focus input or Alt + O to optimize prompts.
🗑️ Trash Bin
Deleted a chat by mistake? Restore your conversations from the built-in Trash Bin.
📊 Word Counter
Track input length in real-time with detailed stats for sentences, paragraphs, and characters.
↕️ Scroll Buttons
Navigate long threads effortlessly with floating arrows to jump to the top or bottom.
🧹 Declutter UI
Hide location info, activity, and premade gems to maximize your sidebar real estate.
⚙️ Control Center
Everything is customizable. Toggle features on/off and clean up the Gemini interface in Settings.
